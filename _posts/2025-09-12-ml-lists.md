**Recommendations:**
- **For Commercial Projects**: Use **YOLOX**, **PP-YOLO**, or **YOLO-NAS** (Apache 2.0).
- **For Open-Source Projects**: GPL-licensed models (YOLOv6/v7) are fine if you embrace Copyleft.
- **Avoid AGPL**: YOLOv8’s AGPL-3.0 imposes strict SaaS/cloud compliance.

**Why Does This Matter?**

- **GPL/AGPL**: Requires sharing derivative code, which may expose proprietary IP.
- **Apache/MIT**: No such restrictions — modify freely and keep code private.

**Models to consider:**
* YOLOv4
* YOLOv5
* YOLOv7: PyTorch and DarkNet implementations
* DAMO-YOLO: PyTorch
* RF-DETR
* RetinaNet
